Target Audience

Personas

Persona 1

Persona Group: Business Owner

Name: Jim Bosche

Business owner looking to involve the company in more local events and network with other business men and women.

Persona 2

Persona Group: Intern Applicant

Name: Michelle McArthur

Intern applicant for the chamber of commerce looking to learn more about the chamber's mission, members, and job and volunteer opportunities.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Jim is interested in joining a local pro business group. He finds the chamber's website and learns about how he can become a member and how it can greatly benefit his business.

Scenario 2

Michelle has an interview for an internship at the Chamber of Commerce coming up and knows that learning more about its mission will give her a good edge. She reads about what the chamber stands for and how they value their members and community work.

Scenario 3

Jim wants to involve his employees and business in more community programs - good publicity and involvement. He finds volunteer opportunities on the website for the Chamber of Commerce that he sends to his employees to encourage attendance.

Scenario 4

Jenny has some complaints about the way some business are being run. She decides the best course of action is to contact the chamber these business are part of and inform of the misconduct. On the chamber of comerce website, she finds the information she needs and feels that this will help others.

Scenario 5

Ken is a web developer looking to improve the website. While exploring, he finds spots that could be more clear and better designed, as well as ways to improve the chamber's image within their own website.
